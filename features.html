Features

Quoda is packed full of features designed to make coding on your smartphone and tablet easier and more productive. Here are just some of the best features you can find on Quoda!

CODE COMPLETION

Get suggestions for what to type next to speed up typing and improve accuracy. Currently only available in HTML - but stay tuned, more languages will be supported soon!

SYNTAX HIGHLIGHTING

Syntax highlighting is supported for a variety of languages, including HTML, XML, CSS, JavaScript, Java, PHP, C, C++, C#, ActionScript, Lisp, Markdown, Haskell and more. Choose from a variety of syntax highlighting themes, and even a variety of fonts!

CLOUD & SERVER CONNECTED

Open, edit and upload files to an unlimited number of FTP/FTPS Servers, and cloud storage like Dropbox and Google Drive

SNIPPETS

Quoda has support for snippets with tabstops, variables, auto-suggestions and tabtriggers/keyboard shortcuts. The app comes with a library of over 500 snippets for various languages, and you can add your own and organise them in folders.

FIND & REPLACE

Quoda includes a fast find and replace system, with support for regular expressions and case sensitivity.

SPECIAL KEYS

Quoda adds extra keys to your keyboard so those hard-to-access symbols are only a click or swipe away.

TABLET OPTIMISED

Quoda has been designed to take advantage of the larger screens on tablet devices, and provides a file browser and even an HTML Live Preview pane right next to your code.
